Campaigns

Redefining how users create, manage, and monitor ad campaigns through an intuitive, automated experience.

Project Overview

The Campaigns project aimed to simplify the process of creating marketing campaigns in a SaaS product. The challenge was to design a dashboard where marketers could easily configure campaign types, pacing, budgets, and creative assets — all while maintaining full visibility of performance in real time.

Problem Statement

Existing workflows were fragmented across multiple tools, causing inefficiency, data loss, and confusion. Users needed a unified solution that could reduce setup time while ensuring better campaign control.

Research & Personas

Marketing Manager

Needs quick setup and automated pacing control to focus on strategy, not manual monitoring.

Performance Analyst

Requires clear performance insights and easy data export for decision-making.

Design Process

  • User interviews and workflow mapping
  • Identifying pain points in campaign setup
  • Wireframing automation logic and dashboard layout
  • Usability testing and iteration

Wireframes & Visual Exploration

Final Design

The final design introduced a clean dashboard interface with a visual progress tracker, live preview, and AI-assisted pacing automation.

Key Outcomes

  • 60% faster campaign setup time
  • Improved collaboration between design and marketing teams
  • Automated pacing reduced manual tracking errors by 40%
