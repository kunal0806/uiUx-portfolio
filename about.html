Hello, I’m Jagriti

I’m a UX/UI designer who turns complexity into clarity — creating digital products that feel human, beautiful, and effortless to use.

My Story

I started my design journey out of curiosity for how people interact with technology — why some products feel natural, while others frustrate us. Over the past 5+ years, that curiosity evolved into a deep passion for creating experiences that empower people and drive business results.

I’ve worked across diverse industries — from healthcare to ad tech — helping brands translate strategy into clear, intuitive digital products. Every project starts with empathy, and ends with impact.

“I believe good design isn’t just what it looks like — it’s how it makes people feel.”

Design Philosophy

Empathy First

I design by understanding people — their emotions, struggles, and goals. Every interaction begins with listening.

Clarity Over Complexity

I simplify information without dumbing it down, ensuring users always feel confident and in control.

Purpose in Every Pixel

Every design choice I make — color, layout, type — serves a reason: to guide, to inform, to inspire.
