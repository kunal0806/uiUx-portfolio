Beyond the Screen,
Creativity Lives Everywhere

When I’m not designing digital experiences, I explore the art of creating in other forms — candles, colors, light, and life. These are the moments that keep me inspired, grounded, and connected to emotion.

My Creative Escapes

Candle Crafting

I hand-pour soy candles, blending fragrances that tell stories of calm, focus, and nostalgia.

Photography

Capturing textures, light, and fleeting moments reminds me how emotion exists in details.

Illustration & Sketching

I sketch characters and moods to stay close to storytelling — the heart of great design.

Journaling

Writing down my thoughts keeps my creativity grounded — reflection fuels clarity.

Travel & Culture

Discovering places, art, and people inspires empathy — the foundation of my UX practice.

DIY Crafts

Creating something tangible brings joy — it reminds me design is about feeling, not pixels.

Why It Matters

These creative explorations shape my approach to UX — they keep empathy alive, remind me to slow down, and help me see beauty in imperfection. Whether I’m blending wax or designing an interface, the intention is the same: to craft experiences that feel human.
