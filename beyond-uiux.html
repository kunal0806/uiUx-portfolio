From Pixels to Wax

My journey into candle making began as a creative escape from screens, a way to engage my senses in the physical world. What started as a hobby quickly evolved into a parallel creative practice.

I approach each candle with the same design thinking I apply to digital products: understanding the user (or in this case, the experiencer), mapping emotional journeys through scent, and crafting beautiful, functional objects that bring joy and comfort.

"In both interfaces and candles, I design for emotion, creating experiences that resonate beyond the visual."
